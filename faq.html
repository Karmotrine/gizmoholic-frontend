What is the status of my order?

You will receive a confirmation e-mail once you ordered your Phone. Once the Phone ships, you will receive a second e-mail that will contain the tracking number for your order allowing you to obtain information on the shipping status of your Phone.

How long will shipping of my Phone take?

Shipping will take approximately 1 to 7 business days depending on your shipping location.

What is the cost of shipping for the Phone?

A shipping fee of P500 will be added per order within Philippines. The shipping fee will be automatically added to your order at the time of order submission. Delivery orders to addresses outside of Philippines are not accepted.

Does the Phone work with any SIM or carrier worldwide?

The Phones work with compatible SIM cards from any Filipino carrier. The Phones may be compatible with other wireless networks around the world. Contact your local service provider for more details.

Where do I get my SIM card?

You can obtain your SIM card from any participating retail locations of your wireless carrier in Philippines

Do Phones work on all Philippines network providers?

Yes

Can I return my Phone?

DOA or Buyer's Remorse

You can return any undamaged Phone if deemed “Dead on Arrival” (DOA) or due to Buyer’s Remorse within 15 days from the date you received the Phone. The Phone must be returned in its original condition along with any original packaging, manuals, accessories, free items and any other components accompanying the Phone.

• Determining if phone is DOA

If we are unable to resolve the issue over the phone, and for you to return the Phone, you must request a Return Merchandise Authorization number and a pre-printed waybill that will be given to you during the call. IF YOU RETURN THE PRODUCTS WITHOUT AN AUTHORIZED RETURN MATERIAL AUTHORIZATION NUMBER AND/OR THE PRE-PRINTED WAYBILL, NEITHER TECH DATA NOR SAMSUNG WILL ISSUE A CREDIT FOR THE RETURNED PHONE. More rigorous hands-on testing will be performed to validate whether or not it is DOA once your Phone is returned to Tech Data. If the Phone is found not to be defective (based on the reason you provided with the return request), your Phone will be returned to you and no credit or refund will be issued. “No fault found” returns do not qualify for Buyer’s Remorse returns. If the Phone is deemed DOA, we will then issue you a credit upon receipt of the Phone. Provided that the Phone is returned in accordance with the terms of this section and is deemed DOA after the inspection set out above, you will receive a phone repair or customer’s protection for the Phone, including any taxes paid, in accordance with Tech Data’s Returns Policy and subject to applicable provincial law. The refund due to you will be processed within 3 to 7 business days based on the original payment method.

Can you use phone while charging?

Yes, you can use your smartphone while charging. There is no danger in using your phone while it's charging. When you use your phone while charging, the battery is charging at a slower rate than normal to allow enough power for the ongoing usage.

I forgot to order something. Can I add it to my existing order?

Unfortunately, you cannot add further items to an order that has already been placed, and you will need to place a second order.
